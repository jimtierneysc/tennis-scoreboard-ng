    Model for a player

    A player may be on a doubles team or a singles team

    A singles team is a special kind of team with only one player. Singles teams represent players in singles matches

    A player may be the first or second server in a match

    A player may be the server of a game

def singles_team!
  team = singles_team
  unless team
    team = Team.new
    team.doubles = false
    team.first_player = self
    team.save!
  end
  team
end
